About

Joe is a research student at Essex University, where he is researching the use of computer vision algorithms running on mobile devices to detect and recognise behaviour of livestock.

Prior to that, he worked in Geospatial Information Systems for the last-mile energy infrastructure business, with brief spells in Net-Zero consultancy as a data wrangler and the heady world of textiles merchantry.

He also maintains Git for Graduates, a book on the effective use of Git that fits on your phone.

When he's not making computers see things or draw lines, he can be found riding motorcycles in East Anglia or paddling kayaks on the Norfolk Broads.
